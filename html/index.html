What kind of coffee drinker are YOU?

1. The Instant Coffee Drinkers

These coffee drinkers are all about the instant gratification life. They want coffee that is good but doesn’t take too long to make. They can’t let their busy lives stop them from having their caffeine, so they stick to what’s quick, delicious and satisfying.

2. The French Press Coffee Lovers

These coffee lovers love a freshly brewed cup of rich French Press Coffee. They often keep the barista tool in their kitchen so they can have their favourite type of coffee at home, at any time.

3. The Flavoured Coffee Drinkers

These coffee drinkers love different flavours blended with their coffee. They might enjoy a cup of simple coffee once in a while but a different flavour is something that really attracts them and satisfies their taste buds. They love trying new flavours and experimenting with their coffee habits.

4. The South Indian Coffee Lovers

Some of these coffee drinkers are so obsessed with authentic South Indian Filter Coffee that they go to South Indian restaurants just for that delicious frothy cup of coffee. They love the rich taste of filter coffee and simply can’t get enough of it.

5. The Hot & Cold Brew Lovers

Some like it hot, while others like it cold. These coffee drinkers brew their coffee in their own time, depending on how light or strong they want it. Sometimes they drink it black and sometimes they add milk or sugar to the brewed concentrate.

6. The Black Coffee Drinkers

These coffee drinkers like to keep it straight and simple. They like their coffee as it is, without any additions. They devour it black without milk or sugar and enjoy every sip of it.

7. The Cold Coffee Lovers

These coffee drinkers like to keep it straight and simple. They like their coffee as it is, These are the kind of coffee drinkers who would always order a cold coffee wherever they go. Goes without saying that it’s their favourite coffee drink, but cold coffee lovers can be obsessive. Sometimes they would have without milk, sometimes they want a frothy glass with milk. Honestly, it tastes great both ways.

8. The Latte Lovers

These coffee drinkers love a frothy, rich cup of latte wherever they go. They love the strong yet smooth taste and creamy texture. It’s their go-to coffee drink and we completely understand why. Who doesn’t love a good latte?
